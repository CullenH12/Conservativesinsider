"This is an attack on America’s identity," said a furious Senator Ted Cruz. "First they take our gas stoves, now they’re coming for our eagles!"

They don’t just want to change the national bird—they want to change what it means to be American." Meanwhile, liberal activists are celebrating the decision, calling the bald eagle a "symbol of outdated nationalism." Some are even demanding that **Mount Rushmore be replaced with a giant mural of Greta Thunberg.** As tensions rise, GOP leaders have vowed to fight this decision in Congress. **Is this the final step in erasing traditional American values?** Stay tuned as this story develops.
