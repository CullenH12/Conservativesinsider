<p>Leaked internal discussions indicate that several advisors have **raised concerns about the bald eagle**, arguing that it is a “dated and overly aggressive” representation of America. <div class="quote">"The bald eagle has served its purpose," said a political strategist aligned with the movement. "It’s time for something fresh, something that reflects 21st-century values."</div>
